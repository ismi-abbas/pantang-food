# Pantang Food Design Notes

## Visual Direction
Warm editorial, inspired by impeccable-style principles: calm hierarchy, intentional whitespace, refined typography, and a restrained palette.

## Palette
- Background: parchment cream
- Text: near-black ink
- Secondary text: warm muted gray
- Accent: terracotta
- Surfaces: soft cream and warm stone tones

## Typography
- Serif for hero and article-style headings
- Sans for UI labels, filters, and metadata
- Monospace for tiny editorial labels and utility text

## Layout Principles
- One dominant story per screen
- Reduce repeated card-grid patterns
- Use cards only when the content truly benefits from containment
- Prefer editorial lists and breathing room for supporting sections
- Make the recipe archive the main interactive system

## Interaction Principles
- Primary CTA should be unmistakable and high contrast
- Filters should stay compact and readable
- Buttons and pills should feel tactile, not flashy
- Mobile should feel like a native app shell

## Quality Bar
- Strong hierarchy at a glance
- Comfortable line lengths
- No placeholder-looking blocks
- Tested contrast for important text and CTA combinations
- Browser-verified polish before shipping
