# Pantang Food Product Brief

## Users
Malay mothers and families looking for warm, practical pantang recipes during the postpartum recovery period.

## Product Purpose
Pantang Food helps users browse, filter, save, and add confinement-friendly recipes in a mobile-first experience that feels calm, warm, and trustworthy.

## Core Goals
- Make the 44-day recovery journey easy to understand
- Help users find recipes quickly by week, benefit, and category
- Make family recipes easy to add and revisit
- Keep the app installable, fast, and touch-friendly on mobile

## Content Principles
- Bilingual copy when it helps clarity
- Respectful, home-cooked tone
- Real photography over generic stock where possible
- Fewer, better sections instead of dense dashboard-style modules

## Anti-Goals
- Do not make the UI feel like a product analytics dashboard
- Do not copy other sites verbatim
- Do not overuse cards, gradients, or loud UI chrome
- Do not bury the main recipe browsing experience
